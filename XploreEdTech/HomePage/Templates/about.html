Our Aim

Our aim is to make 'Xplore EdTech' site where educators from around the globe can easily access the Educational Technology that best suits the age-group and the subject they teach. By implementing these tools in the classroom, students will interact and engage giving them the learning experience as well.
We want our educators to spark with educational technolgies in their classroom!

What We Do

We provide Educational Technology for educators to implement in the classroom by providng them with the tools and how it can be used. All guidance are provided in the form of links and educators can access them making it less time consuming. In addition, worksheets can also be purchased that have been uploaded by our fellow users.

Donation Process

By purchasing the worksheets, all income gained through this will help under-priviledged students who are finding it hard to have the basic needs to study.
We are partnered with Organization 01 & Organization 02 for this cause.
